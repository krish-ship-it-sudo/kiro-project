# CheckInSafe 🛡️

**Your personal safety companion for peace of mind**

CheckInSafe is a simple yet powerful web application designed to help you stay accountable for your safety. Whether you're traveling alone, meeting someone new, or just want to let yourself (or others) know you've arrived safely, CheckInSafe has you covered.

## The Problem

We've all been in situations where we tell someone "I'll text you when I get home" and then forget. Or we're traveling alone and wish we had a simple way to track our safe arrivals. CheckInSafe solves this by creating a gentle accountability system that reminds you to check in when you reach your destination.

## How It Works

1. **Set Your Destination**: Enter where you're going (home, office, friend's place, etc.)
2. **Set Expected Arrival**: Choose what time you expect to arrive
3. **Start Check-In**: The app begins monitoring your check-in
4. **Confirm Safety**: When you arrive, click "I reached safely"
5. **Get Alerts**: If you don't check in on time, the app triggers an alert

## Features

✅ **Simple Interface**: Clean, intuitive design that anyone can use  
✅ **Time Monitoring**: Automatically tracks if you've checked in on time  
✅ **Alert System**: Visual alerts when check-in time is exceeded  
✅ **Check-In History**: View all your past check-ins and their status  
✅ **Offline Storage**: All data stored locally in your browser  
✅ **Mobile Friendly**: Responsive design works on any device  
✅ **No Sign-Up**: Start using immediately, no account required

## Technology Stack

- **HTML5**: Semantic markup for structure
- **CSS3**: Modern styling with gradients and animations
- **JavaScript (ES6+)**: Pure vanilla JS, no frameworks
- **LocalStorage API**: Client-side data persistence

## Getting Started

1. Download or clone this repository
2. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser
3. Start creating check-ins!

No installation, no dependencies, no build process. Just open and use.

## Use Cases

- **Solo Travel**: Check in when you reach your hotel or destination
- **Late Night Commutes**: Confirm you got home safely
- **First Dates**: Set a check-in time for peace of mind
- **Business Trips**: Track your arrivals at different locations
- **Daily Routine**: Build a habit of safety awareness

## Privacy & Security

CheckInSafe is completely private:
- All data stays in your browser (LocalStorage)
- No data sent to any server
- No tracking or analytics
- No account or personal information required
